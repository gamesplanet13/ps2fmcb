⚠️ Important Notes

  • Do not delete FMCB / OPL / ELF / system (blue icon) files.
  • USB works with PS2 Fat & Slim (7xxxx / 9xxxx). Plug directly into PS2 (no hub/splitter).

⚠️ Chip Condition (Read Before Order)

  • 🟢 FMCB & FunTuna both work without any modding chip.
  • 🟢 If your PS2 has Matrix or Sumo Lite chip → usually OK.
  • 🔴 If your PS2 has a DVD Boot Chip → 99% chances FMCB/FunTuna will not work.
  • 👉 In that case, chip wires must be disconnected (requires opening PS2). Message us for the step-by-step guidance video.
